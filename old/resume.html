Work Experience

During my time with IBM, I have accomplished and learned many great skills such as tool development, web automation, application/database scripting and dealing with big data in an efficient and secure manner. One of the data collection tools that I developed and shipped internally is currently saving IBM approximately $1000 annually per employee who uses it. I can further elaborate in an interview setting how this number is calculated.
Further accomplishments include my backup/restoration automation and data verification automation which have been delivered and are also being used internally. My automation made significant advances to IBM's initiatives in creating pipeline software to increase testing efficiency and hence produce a more continuous development environment.


During my time with WIRE (Waterloo Intelligent Robotics Experiments), I completed some valuable research on the history and future of high speed cameras. I also researched the environmental impact of these types of cameras. My research paper was valued highly by the Engineering team.


I worked with Onprint over the summer doing graphic design work using Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator. It was a very interesting position as I was working directly on graphic design that local companies and start-ups requested. My graphic design ranged from brochures to business cards to menus to window decals.
