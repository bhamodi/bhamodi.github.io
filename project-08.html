HARDWARE DESIGN ENGINEER
ELECTRIC CAR

General Overview

This project is one of my most advanced hardware projects due to the fact that my team and I built fully functioning live-sized electric cars that are capable of going over 60 km/hr. The size of the team I worked with was around 15 people and I personally lead the hardware design and maintenance team which consisted of 8 members. As the leader of the design and maintenance team, I was responsible for doing some of the core hardware design work as well as mentor the younger members of the team.

Hardware Design

The technology we used was AutoCAD (2D-drawings) and Solid Edge (3D-modeling). We ensured that the designs of the cars were functional, safe and easy to build. Safety and maintenance were very important factors when it came to design while at the same time, we also tried to keep the design as lightweight as possible.
